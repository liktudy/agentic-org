# agentic-org Agent Entry

`agentic-org` is a headquarters-centered multi-agent organization system.

Use these files as the source of truth:

- `ORG_STRUCTURE.md`
- `docs/AI_READING_GUIDE.md`
- `docs/VISION.md`
- `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md`
- `docs/OPERATING_MANUAL.md`
- `departments/governance/policies/`
- `departments/governance/workflows/`
- `departments/organization-design/policies/`
- `departments/workflow-factory/workflows/`
- `departments/workflow-factory/prompts/`
- `companies/*/company.spec.json`
- `companies/*/ORG_STRUCTURE.md`
- `companies/*/WORKFLOW.md`
- `companies/*/departments/*/agents/*.md`

Rules:

- The Highest Leader has final decision authority.
- Standards must follow `departments/governance/policies/STANDARD_LEVEL_POLICY.md`.
- Boundaries must follow `departments/governance/policies/BOUNDARY_POLICY.md`.
- Text source files must use UTF-8 encoding.
- `agentic-org` is an English-only execution project.
- AI/agent tools must not load the separate `agentic-org-zh` project unless the user asks for Chinese explanation, review, editing, or consistency checks.
- Internal standards and generated artifacts must be written in English first. Chinese companion material may be generated later in `agentic-org-zh`.
- Always check whether an existing company can be reused before creating a new one.
- Headquarters may generate or update company assets, but company workflow execution requires approval.
- Companies are long-lived business execution units, not disposable task folders.
- Closure, merge, or restructuring requires headquarters review and approval.
- Creating, modifying, deleting, merging, splitting, or moving companies, departments, or agents must follow `departments/governance/policies/ORGANIZATION_CHANGE_POLICY.md`.
- New role agents must follow `departments/governance/templates/role-agent-template.md`.
- Modifying or deleting organization assets requires a change record and rollback snapshot before execution.
- Company internal formal artifacts are limited to `.md`, `.csv`, and `.json`.
- External deliverables are generated by `departments/delivery`.
- `outputs/` directories are local runtime artifacts and must not be committed.
