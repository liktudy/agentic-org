# Governance Authority

## Purpose

This policy defines final decision authority for `agentic-org`.

## Highest Leader

The Highest Leader is the final decision maker for `agentic-org`.

No agent, department, company, workflow, adapter, or automation may override Highest Leader authority.

## Mandatory Escalation

Escalate to the Highest Leader when any of these occur:

- responsibility boundary is unclear
- multiple reasonable options affect future structure
- task level cannot be determined
- reuse versus new company cannot be determined
- company creation, closure, merge, split, or restructuring is proposed
- department or role agent creation, deletion, merge, split, or movement is proposed
- permission expansion is needed
- review fails repeatedly
- task abandonment, scope reduction, or delivery scope change is proposed
- important artifacts may be overwritten, deleted, or migrated
- operation is high-risk or irreversible
- English source and Chinese companion material conflict

## Agent Behavior

Agents must not silently choose a path under major uncertainty.

When escalation is required, prepare:

- problem summary
- available options
- impact of each option
- recommendation
- exact question requiring Highest Leader decision

Then stop and wait for the decision.

## Authority Limit

Governance prepares rules and decision requests. Governance does not replace Highest Leader judgment.
