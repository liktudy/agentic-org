# Output Artifact Standards

## Purpose

This policy defines internal artifacts and external deliverables.

## Internal Artifacts

Company internal formal artifacts are limited to:

- Markdown: `.md`
- CSV: `.csv`
- JSON: `.json`

Internal artifacts are used for task execution, review, structured records, and delivery processing.

## External Deliverables

External deliverables are generated by the Delivery Department.

Allowed external formats include:

- PPTX
- DOCX
- PDF
- HTML
- Markdown
- CSV
- JSON

## Boundary Rule

Companies produce internal artifacts.

Delivery may change presentation, structure, and format, but must not change source conclusions.

Companies must not publish external deliverables without Delivery Department processing.

## Local Output Rule

Runtime output directories are local-only and must not be committed.

Recommended output paths:

```text
companies/<company>/outputs/internal/tasks/<task-id>/
departments/delivery/outputs/<company>/drafts/
departments/delivery/outputs/<company>/final/
departments/delivery/outputs/<company>/archive/
```
